Work Experience

Greenway Health, LLC

Software Engineer II

July 2015 - Present

I joined Greenway Health shortly after graduating from UChicago and started as an Associate Software Engineer. Since then, I have been promoted to Software Engineer and now hold the title of Software Engineer II.

I am currently a team lead and work on the Greenway Patient Portal and Messaging solutions, used by millions of provider and patient users. I take part in full stack development in an Agile Scrum environment. Our stack includes .NET MVC and Web API, Angular, SQL Server, Redis, and RabbitMQ.

In addition to programming, I participate in peer code reviews for coding conventions, design, and unit tests. I mentor junior software engineers and assist our quality assurance engineers in automation testing with Protractor. During meetings, I design the code and system architectures for our applications for scalability and maintainability. I collaborate frequently with other engineers and product managers.

My interest in cybersecurity led me to become the company's first security ambassador, to serve as the liaison between the product team and the company's cybersecurity team. In this role, I run application security scans, identify and prioritze vulnerabilities based on manual and automated testing, and communicate with both the product and cybersecurity teams to mitigate vulnerabilities more efficiently.

University of Chicago Research Computing Center

Technical Support

June 2014 - June 2015

As a technical support assistant, I managed account requests for the Midway supercomputer cluster and responded to troubleshooting issues for research groups.

I also wrote scripts and monitored cluster usage. I also worked on various student projects to improve cluster monitoring and create custom applications for users.

I also assisted in coordinating RCC events.
